Jackson Fix

Jackson with his cousin's cat, Misty, acting like the Bond-villain he was meant to be.

Despite only doing so over the last five years, Jackson Fix has acquired a morbid curiousity for ill-fated or poorly-made films. Born on December 29th, 1998 in Ottawa, Jackson primarily grew up with a passion for sports, primarily hockey and baseball. Jackson's appreciation for films comes from his experience at Carleton University. While he earned a BA in History, he took a few film courses on subjects ranging from Film Theory to Stanley Kubrick. He currently studies at Algonquin Collge in Library and Information Technology while cheering on his Ottawa Senators and Baltimore Orioles. Despite the bio image, he is a dog person who grew up with a Husky named Kirov (1993-2001) and a Beagle named Guinness (2007-2025)
